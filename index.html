About Me

This is the story about a girl just trying to find passion and purpose in what she does. I am hopeful that this is IT! I have a bachelors and MBA in Internation Business, and somehow I have gotten pidgeon-holed into Finance despite my lack of strong academic evidence in the same. I would have been more thrilled working in something artistic, such as fashion or interior design. I also have a passion for real estate investment and believe that will be way to create wealth. Meanwhile, Web Development is my current creative tool of choice and I think I'll love it. Wish me luck!
